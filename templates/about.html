About Globe News

Our Mission

Globe News delivers accurate, timely, and comprehensive news coverage from Rwanda and around the world. We combine automated news aggregation with human editorial oversight to provide readers with reliable, unbiased information that helps them understand the world around them.

Our Story

Founded in 2026, Globe News emerged from a vision to create a modern news platform that leverages technology while maintaining journalistic integrity. What started as a simple news aggregator has grown into a comprehensive news source covering 30+ RSS feeds across multiple categories and languages.

Our Coverage

We curate news from over 30 trusted sources including:

  • International: BBC, Reuters, Associated Press, CNN, Al Jazeera, The Guardian
  • Technology: TechCrunch, Wired
  • Business: Bloomberg, KT Press
  • Rwandan News: IGIHE, New Times Rwanda, BBC Kinyarwanda, RBA Rwanda

Our Technology

Globe News is powered by a modern tech stack including FastAPI backend with Redis caching, Flask frontend, and persistent SQLite database. We've archived over 1,800 articles with full content extraction for 30% of them, ensuring readers get comprehensive news coverage.

Our Team

Globe News combines advanced AI technology with dedicated human editors who ensure every story meets our standards for accuracy and relevance. Our team works around the clock to bring you the latest news as it happens.
